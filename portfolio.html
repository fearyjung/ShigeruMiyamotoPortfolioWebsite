Portfolio
Over the years, Shigeru has made over 160+ Games in total, and a total of 11 games of Mario Series. The character Mario, first made its debut in the very first Donkey Kong Video/Arcade Game. After that Shigeru started designing a hit game after hit, his work became so popular that it is played all around the world!!
